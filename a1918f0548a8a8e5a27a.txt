Welcome to MyPad!

MyPad is a lightweight and intuitive text editor designed for simplicity and productivity.  
Use the panel on the left to manage your notes, and the editor on the right to write and save your thoughts.  

- Click "New Notes" to create a new entry  
- Select any note to view or edit it  
- Use "Save" to keep your work  
- "Print" your notes instantly  
- "Delete" removes notes from both the app and your disk

Your notes are automatically organized and stored safely in your local system.
